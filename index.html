Hi, I'm Barbora

I was born and raised in the beautiful city of Kaunas, the heart of Lithuania. Nature and travel, different cultures always been an inspiration to me as I grew up surrounded by beautiful pagan traditions. In 2008, after I graduated as a Designer and Manufacturer of Contemporary metal art works in Lithuania, I realised make-up and fashion - just beauty in general was my passion and it was the path for me to pursue. Sweden is the home of grand fashion brands, inspiring designers, photographers and make-up artist whose work I look up to. I find the Swedish culture resembling to my Lithuanian spirit.

2012 I stared my career of studding makeup artistry in one of the pioneer and leading makeup school in Sweden. After my school my travel in beauty, fashion, and people development started. I began a makeup artist and hairstylist in Stockholms’ F12 studio’s, worked with newly established Swedish clothing designers such as ”Resteröds“, “Linn“, ”Emma Eddy Andersson“, “I Started Something I couldn‘t finish“ and “Gooseberry Lay & Co”. As well as this, the skills that I have acquired throughout the years range from fashion styling, to people training and development few to mention. What fascinate me most about my work is the moment of now and the unique people I have pleasure to work with.
